Our Philosophy

Three Layers of Trust Architecture

We don't catch cheaters. We create conditions where cheating is harder to sustain and healthy transparency is easier to choose.

Commitment Verification

Structured conversation prompts covering monogamy definitions, emotional affair boundaries, and transparency expectations. AI identifies semantic gaps before they become fractures.

  • Living relationship contracts
  • Expectation mapping surveys
  • Quarterly alignment reviews

Behavioral Transparency

Voluntary logging of social interactions and communication rhythms. Pattern asymmetries surface naturally without surveillance or content access.

  • Mutual presence ledger
  • Communication rhythm analysis
  • Randomized trust checks

Post-Breach Recovery

If trust is broken, structured 90-day transparency protocols help verify genuine reconciliation without enabling controlling behaviors.

  • Reconstruction mode
  • Accountability partner network
  • Pattern-based verification
